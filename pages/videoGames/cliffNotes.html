Cliff Notes is a 2D puzzle game where the player must help Cliff, a stick figure with little to no self-preservation,
reach the flag at the end of each level. This game was made by Team 11 in three months in Professor Mell's AI for
Game Programming class at UCF and won the class award for Best Artistic Direction in Fall 2024.
