Spherical Gravity Test

Director Hall challenged me to make my own spherical gravity system, so I took up the challenge, and over around eight hours, I was able to get a modular demo working. Although it was a challenge, I found it fun to implement the player movement, the camera system, and especially the modular gravity manager that allows any number of planets and objects to be added.
