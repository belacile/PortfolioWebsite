"Compliance"

  "Comply" (Version One of "Compliance")

  Final Portfolio Reflection and "Compliance"

This short story was created for the Spring 2024 semester of Advanced Fiction Writing Workshop with Professor
Brown. This story follows the struggle of a computer science student, along with his sidekick Ducky, who
procrastinates on a difficult coding assignment until the code starts speaking back to him.

The Final Portfolio Reflection is a reflection on my peers' critique of "Compliance," addressed to Professor Brown,
and includes a revised version of the story.
