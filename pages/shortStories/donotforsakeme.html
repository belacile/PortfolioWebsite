  "Do Not Forsake Me"

This short story was created for the Spring 2024 semester of Advanced Fiction Writing Workshop with Professor
Brown. This story follows a pumpkin-man named Kynn as he attempts to bring back a pet from the dead as the past
catches up with him.

Although "Do Not Forsake Me" has its flaws, it was the most well-recieved story among my peers—some even
staying after class to ask me detailed questions about the strange fantasy world I created for this story.
