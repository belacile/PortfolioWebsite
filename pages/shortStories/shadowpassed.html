"Shadow Passed"

  ShadowPassed.pdf

This short story was created for the Fall 2023 semester of Fiction Writing Workshop with Professor Sanz. This story follows Fabian, a cursed ex-bard, who struggles with an inner demon that he fears will tear apart his life.

Heavily inspired by Dungeons & Dragons, this story manifested when I wondered what would happen if a character left their party because of a horrible curse he feared would put them in danger. I can certainly see my growth since this story, and I've thought of ways I could improve the comedy, combat, and description in future pieces like this one.
